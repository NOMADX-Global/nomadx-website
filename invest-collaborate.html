The Market Opportunity

The Digital Nomad Economy

  • 35+ million digital nomads globally as of 2024
  • Projected 1 billion location-independent workers by 2035
  • $787 billion annual spending power of nomadic workforce
  • Average income of $75k-150k per nomad annually
  • Growing demand for community-centric living and working spaces

Real Estate + Hospitality Evolution

  • Coliving market expected to reach $13.9 billion by 2030
  • Shift from traditional hotels to experience-driven stays
  • Increasing preference for flexible, membership-based models
  • Premium pricing for community and curated experiences
  • Stable recurring revenue from long-term stays vs. transient bookings

Cultural Shift

  • Millennial and Gen-Z prioritize experiences over ownership
  • Remote work normalizing permanent location independence
  • Desire for belonging in an increasingly disconnected world
  • Rejection of traditional 9-5 and fixed-location lifestyle
  • Quest for purpose-driven work and authentic community
