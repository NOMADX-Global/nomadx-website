For Property Owners

Transform your property into a thriving hub for the global nomad community. We handle everything from operations to community building, while you enjoy stable returns and purpose-driven impact.

What We're Looking For

  • Properties in desirable nomad destinations (urban or nature settings)
  • Minimum capacity of 100+ guests
  • Space for coworking and community activities
  • Alignment with our sustainability and community values

What We Offer

  • Professional Management - Full-service operations including maintenance, staffing, and guest experience
  • Brand Power - Access to our global community and marketing reach
  • Technology Platform - Booking system, member management, and operational tools
  • Revenue Guarantee - Minimum occupancy guarantees and performance-based upside
  • Design Support - Space optimization and community-centric design consultation
  • Shared Economics - Flexible partnership models including revenue share and lease arrangements

Partnership Models

  • Management Agreement - You own, we operate
  • Lease Partnership - We lease your property with guaranteed monthly income
  • Joint Venture - Co-investment in property development or acquisition
  • Franchise Model - Licensed use of NOMADX brand and systems

Partnership Process

1. Initial Inquiry

Reach out via the form below. Tell us about your property, business, or organization and how you envision partnering with NOMADX.

2. Discovery Call

We'll schedule a conversation to understand your goals, explore mutual fit, and discuss potential partnership models.

3. Site Visit & Assessment

For property partnerships, we'll conduct a thorough assessment including location analysis, property inspection, and financial modeling.

4. Proposal & Negotiation

We'll present a detailed partnership proposal with financial projections, operational plans, and terms for your review and negotiation.

5. Agreement & Launch

Once aligned, we'll finalize legal agreements and begin the onboarding process—typically 2-6 months from preparation to official launch.

Our Partnership Values

We're selective about our partnerships because quality matters. We look for partners who share:

  • Community Focus - Genuine care for creating spaces where people thrive
  • Quality Standards - Commitment to excellent guest experience and operations
  • Sustainability - Environmental consciousness and responsible practices
  • Long-term Vision - Interest in building lasting value, not quick profits
  • Cultural Sensitivity - Respect for local communities and cultures
  • Innovation Mindset - Openness to new ideas and continuous improvement
